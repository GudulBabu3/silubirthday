The story of a princess and a sorcerer

_______________________

I once met a princess

In a dungeon far, far away,

Where tyranny ruled

For day after day.

.

The tyrant, an evil sorcerer,

With a cold, stern heart,

Casting spells of dismay,

Tearing dreams apart.

.

In the dungeon of despair,

Darkness loomed,

The princess imprisoned,

her spirit consumed.

.

But a prince,

so gallant and wise,

Saw through the sorcerer's

perfect disguise.

.

He, a passerby

with a heart so true,

Felt a calling,

a mission he must pursue.

.

As the tyrant

ventured to a distant shore,

The prince seized the moment,

swift and sure.

.

He approached the princess

with eyes so kind,

To break the spell,

and free her mind.

.

"Come, fair princess,

let me show,

A realm of sunshine,

Butterflies and Rainbow,

.

Where birds sing

to the tunes of joy

And Dreams are alive

Free from the sorcerer's ploy.‚Äù

.

He showed her the wonders,

one by one,

A world forgotten,

but not undone.

.

Yet, the princess,

lost in the tyrant's deceit,

Couldn't remember

her royal seat.

.

The prince, undeterred,

pondered a plan,

To awaken her memories,

like grains of sand.

.

In a magical ritual,

under the open sky,

He brewed a potion,

warm and spry.

.

A concoction of awakening,

a potion so grand,

A cup of coffee,

the elixir in his hand.

.

He handed her the cup,

with love in the air,

As she sipped,

her memories repair.

.

The taste of the potion,

like a gentle kiss,

Awakened the princess,

from the tyrant's abyss.

.

No longer a captive,

no longer a slave,

She remembered she was a princess,

bold and brave.

.

So here's to the princess,

with courage so true,

Who broke free from darkness,

and love she knew.
